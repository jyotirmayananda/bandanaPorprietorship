## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated reddish-purple (#C0397A), conveying energy, ambition, and boldness.
- Background color: Light pink (#F4E5EB), offering a soft, modern backdrop that won't compete with other visual elements.
- Accent color: Burgundy (#801E4A) for buttons and active states, enhancing focus.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, is chosen for its modern and neutral appearance, making it suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clean, modern icons relevant to business and proprietorship themes.
- A clean, single-page layout that is easy to navigate and understand.
